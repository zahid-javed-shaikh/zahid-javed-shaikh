### About

I build end-to-end BI and data engineering solutions on the modern Microsoft data stack, turning messy, scattered data into governed, performant products that teams actually use to make decisions.

Most of my recent work sits at the intersection of three things: Microsoft Fabric, Databricks, and Power BI. I'm currently spending a lot of time on how generative AI tooling can shorten the painful part of migrating legacy pipelines into Fabric Lakehouse and Databricks Delta and MLflow environments.
